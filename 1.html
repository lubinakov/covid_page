Country Covid-19 Statistics:

This webpage enables you to have a quick access to up-to-date covid-19 related information for any country. By selecting state you will be redirected to World Health Organization webpage with covid-19 statistics for the state you selected.


Get Travel and Covid-19 Information via U.S. Embassy webpage

If you are interested in more detailed information related to current travel protocols, you can check whether there is U.S. Embassy in the selected country. Provided, there is one, with one click you will be redirected there so you can get travel informations.


Download Your Temperature Tracker

As many countries requires tracking of your temperature upon arrival, you will find a section "Your Temperature Tracker" with a free pdf form for 14-day temperature tracking. You can download the form with your name and dates and provide it to local authorities if requested.
